MY PROJECT

The Complaint Management System is a platform that is designed to address the issue of complaints in Barangay Alicia, Quezon City. A platform that allows residents to make a complaint or report instances of breaking local laws around the Barangay or anything that is considered unethical that may negatively affect the residents living around the area. The system will collect the information about an offending individual or situation, including the location on where the individual or the situation happened, the time of the complaint, and the reason for complaining. Moreover, the residents can take photos as evidence of the alleged troublemaker/s or situation. The collected data will then be forwarded to the Barangay officials, who will investigate the complaint and take actions regarding the violation. The system will reduce the instances of violation that may affect other residents living in Barangay Alicia, Quezon City so it can foster a safer and more cooperative community within the Barangay
